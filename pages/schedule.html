Timeline for Your Project

We aim to launch your website quickly, but we will need input from you to complete it. To make this easy, we’ve streamlined the onboarding process to make things as clear as possible so we can get to work as fast as possible.

Please submit all required forms and materials (logo, images, content, etc.) within one week of the initial onboarding call.

  • Onboarding 1 Week

    During onboarding, we’ll get to know your goals, target audience, and vision for the project. This step helps us gather all essential information and ensures we’re aligned with your expectations (I will send you a form and checklist so everything gets covered). After that, you can submit any content or images to be used on the website into a shared folder on Google Drive.

  • Design 1 week

    I will create initial design concepts, focusing on visuals, layout, and brand aesthetics. This stage is all about crafting a unique, user-friendly experience that reflects your brand’s identity.

  • Design Review 1 Week

    You’ll have a week to review the design concepts and share feedback. This collaborative step allows us to refine the design, ensuring it meets your standards before moving forward.

  • Development 2 Weeks

    Once the design is reviewed and approved, we transition to development, turning your vision into a functional, responsive website. I handle coding, integration, and performance optimization.

  • Final Review 1 Week

    Before going live, we take a week to conduct a final review. This is an opportunity to check out the physical version of your website and make any last-minute requests or adjustments to ensure every detail is perfect.

  • Going Live! 1 Week

    After final approvals, your site goes live! We’ll handle the launch process, monitor the site closely, and ensure a smooth, successful debut.
