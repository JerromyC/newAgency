How long does it take?

Timeline for Your Project

We are dedicated to getting your website live as quickly as possible, but please keep in mind that we will need information from you before we can finish. We have streamlined the onboarding process to make things easier for everyone involved and we suggest that you fill out all of the required forms and upload the necessary deliverables (logo, images, content, etc.) within one week of the start date.

The more effecient the communication, the better the results.

Onboarding ~ 1 week

You will recieve access to a Google Drive that not only contains helpful documentation, but is the hub we will use to share deliverables

Design ~ 1 - 2 weeks

This phase of the project is vital as we narrow down on the core features and functionality of your website before we move to build it out.

Development ~ 1 - 2 weeks

Once you check off on the design, development begins. At this point, our main goal is to get the site live, so any revisions will be made AFTER hosting.
